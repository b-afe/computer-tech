Resilience

Resilience is the ability of an individual to cope and adapt in times of struggle. When faced with obstacles, whether that be failure, financial setbacks, emotional issues etc, resilience is the ability to overcome these challenges and bounce back. When problems become the adversity in life and when hardships involving tragedy, relationships, health, work or school arise, what will you do? How will you respond? Will stress and fear paralyze you or will you take action? Individuals with resilience have learned to deal with their problems efficiently and fight back. This is key to success.


Building Resilience.

Resilience, like any other skill can be developed. Levels of resilience can be built on several factors.

1. Have a positive self image.

Be confident, and know your strengths and weaknesses. Self-awareness will be crucial towards overcoming challenges mentally, emotionally and actually.

2. Surround yourself with support.

Ensure there are positive influences in your life that you can go to when you need help. When times get tough, a shoulder to cry on, encouragement and reassurance will help you get through it.

3. Make SMART Goals.

Being able to make specific, measurable, attainable, realistic and timely goals will create order and organization in your life. Moreover, it is a good habit to have, so when faced with problems, you can easily create new plans and adapt.

4. Manage your emotions effectively.

This can be done through simple breathing exercises, yoga, meditation etc. Whatever works for you. Ensure that you can remain calm in stressful situations, and maintain a level head, so that you can think and problem solve efficiently.

5. Refine your communication and problem solving skills.

Being able to communicate properly and think on your feet is important towards overcoming challenges in life.


Building resilience will take time and dedication. Changing your mindset and building healthy habits will happen overtime, if you work towards it. Keep in mind that the end goal is to improve your mentality and face challenges with confidence and a critical mind, which will help you no matter where you are, and no matter where you go.
