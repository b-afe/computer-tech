Project Overview

Computer Systems

For this assignment, students prepared a presentation on hardware associated with a desktop computer system. They talked about topics such as the purpose of the components, history, name brands, and more. Students also created a handout summarizing the information on the presentation.

Project Overview

Computer Hardware Familiarization

In groups of 3, students created a tutorial explaining how to disassemble and reassemble a desktop computer. Students also drew a diagram of the inside and outside of the computer, and included photos of the process. Students made notes of observations during the project, and wrote a reflection on how well they worked.

Project Overview

Home Network Design

To learn about networking and the factors that must be considered when creating a network plan, students were given the task to create a home network system for a family with 5 TVs, 2 desktop computers, 2 laptop, a printer, 2 ipads and several iphones. Students researched networking devices such as routers, modems, network adapters and more. They also planned a budget, created a network planning manual and wrote a justification for their work.

Project Overview

Office Network Design

To learn about networking and the factors that must be considered when creating a network plan, students were given the task to create an office network for a building with 5 all-purpose desktop computers, a regular printer, an office printer, a workstation computer and a file server. Students researched networking devices such as switches, routers, modems, wire bridges and more. They also planned a budget, created a network planning manual and wrote a justification for their work.
