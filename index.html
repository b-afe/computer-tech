About


What is computer technology / computer engineering?

Computer technology is the study and practice of activities involving computers and other devices. It involves the investigation of electric circuits, computer and software, network planning and programming. Students learn to use technology to efficiently manage, process and communicate information. They develop leadership skills necessary for the real world.

The development of computer technology encourages innovation and inspires students to take on leadership roles and discover their potential in an encouraging work environment.
